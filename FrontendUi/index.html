Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for pension?

You can apply for pension by registering on our portal and submitting the required documents through your dashboard.

What documents are required?

Required documents include proof of identity, service records, bank account details, and retirement order.

How long does processing take?

Standard processing time is 30 days from the date of complete document submission.

How will I receive my pension?

Pensions are directly credited to your registered bank account on the first week of every month.

What if I miss a payment?

Contact our support team immediately through the portal or helpline. We'll investigate and resolve within 48 hours.
